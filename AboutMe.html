ABOUT ME

Hi & Assalamualaikum! I’m Nur Aleeya Natasha, but most people call me Aleeya or Tasha. At home, I go by Adik or Mok. It really depends on the vibe. I'm an ambivert: quiet and thoughtful, but silly and loud once I’m comfortable around you.

I was born on 13 December 2005 in Kuala Lumpur, at Hospital Kuala Lumpur (HKL). I’m the youngest of four siblings, and being the “adik” definitely comes with its own kind of chaos, laughter, and lots of love.

I’ve always loved capturing moments whether it’s a random candid or an aesthetic angle, photography and videography have become a part of me. I'm also known for being a little clumsy (and honestly, I’ve accepted it as part of my charm).

Some of the things that make me feel alive:

  • Cooking—even if it doesn’t turn out perfect, the process is always fulfilling.
  • Feeding others and seeing them smile from something I’ve made.
  • Laughing at the little things, even long after the moment has passed.
  • Exploring cafés and beautiful corners of the city for inspiration.

MY HOBBY 👩‍🍳

Cooking has always been something I love. a passion that brings me quiet joy. Even though it can be tricky at times, and not every dish turns out perfect, I still enjoy every part of the process. From chopping the ingredients to sautéing them gently, there’s a kind of peace in the rhythm of it all. But my favorite part? Sharing the food with others. There’s nothing more fulfilling than seeing people enjoy what I’ve made. I started cooking when I was in Year 6. Back then, I only knew how to cook sardines and eggs. But now, I can proudly say I’ve mastered almost all traditional Malay dishes.

QOTD: Do what you love ♥️ even the smallest joy can brighten your day and someone else's too
